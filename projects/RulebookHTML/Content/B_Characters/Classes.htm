Classes

This is a description of classes, what they are, what they do, and their classification.
Classes are special abilities and powers that go beyond skills and attributes. No application of training a skill can gain you the abilities from classes, they are above and beyond the capabilities of normal people. Classes truly define characters and their difference from normal people. Classes are ranked 1-5. No class is inherent, and the default is that a character has no class ranks. Each rank of a class gives greater control over the domain of the class, though specifics are up to imagination. The classes are broadly defined into 3 categories, and from there, each category has a list of available classes.
