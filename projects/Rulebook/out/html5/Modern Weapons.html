Modern Weapons

Modern weapons refers to firearms and explosives.

Header Description
STR Minimum numerical strength required to use the weapon.
Hand Hands required to wield the weapon.
Cost Cost in gold pieces to use the item.
Ammo Amount of ammo in the weapon before a reload is needed.
FT Fatigue cost to use the weapon in combat.
Dmg Damage of the weapon it deals when attacking an opponent.
CRIT Damage multiplier in case the player gets a superbly critical attack.
Range Maximum range of the weapon, using the weapon beyond this range is harder.
AP Armor piercing, the amount of damage reduction the weapon ignores.
