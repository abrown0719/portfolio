Traveling and Adventure

This is a short overview of traveling and adventure.

Travel and adventure is important to the game. In between moments of action, every other things falls into traveling and adventure. Most things within adventure and travel scenarios are simple checks. They do not have to be resolved by paying attention to time.

This is the time to challenge all the skills that aren't necessary for combat, and to include things such as debates and mysteries.
