Rounds, Turns, and Actions

This is a discussion on what Rounds, Turns, and Actions are.

Combat occurs in rounds, each one taking approximately 10 seconds of game-time. Each round, every character gets a turn within the round, where they take actions. Actions are things that the characters can do. Every character gets to do a number of actions that are equal to their Speed. Actions include things such as running, attacking, or using abilities.
