Armor and Shields

Armor and shields refers to any type of protection worn. Armor is worn over the body, shields are wielded and can be dropped quickly.

Header Description
STR Strength required to wield this armor or shield.
Cost Cost in gold pieces to buy this piece of equipment.
DR Damage reduction addition of the armor or shield.
Skill Modifier Negative modifier to all Dexterity controlled skills as a consequence of wearings or using this particular piece of equipment.
Block Bonus Shield bonus to any active check to block attacks.
