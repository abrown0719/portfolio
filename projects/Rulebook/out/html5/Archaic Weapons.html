Archaic Weapons

Archaic weapons refer to melee weapons and non-firearms that are out-of-use in the modern era.

Header Description
STR Numerical requirement for Strength to use the weapon.
Hand How many hands it requires to wield the weapon.
Cost The amount of money in gold pieces it requires to buy the weapon.
FT How much fatigue it costs to use the weapon in combat.
Dmg How much damage the weapon deals when it hits someone.
CRIT A multiplier for the damage if the character gets an incredible hit.
Type The type of weapon: B=Blunt, S=Sharp.
AP Armor Piercing, how much damage reduction the weapon ignores on target.
Range The maximum range of the weapon, it is harder to use beyond this range.
