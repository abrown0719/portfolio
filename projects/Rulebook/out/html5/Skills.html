Skills

This is a description of what a skill is, and what they do.

Skills are an abstraction of a characters trained abilities. While atributes are untrained and are innate in the character, skills are gained over time. Skills are measured 1-20 like attributes, and without training, each starts at 0. Each skill is controlled by an attribute, and this means that a skill cannot go above the number that its controlling attribute is. If an attribute is at 10, any skill controlled by that attribute cannot go above 10.
