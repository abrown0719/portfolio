Vision and Light

Light sources provide artificial light where there was none, and is important for characters that cannot see in the dark. Each source has a radius of light it provides, past that darkness makes sight impossible.

Light Source Light Radius
Candle 5 ft.
Torch 20 ft.
Lantern 30 ft.
Lightbulb 40 ft.
Electric Lantern 50 ft.
Spotlight Lantern 100 ft. Cone
Fog Light 200 ft. Cone
Floodlight 200 ft.
