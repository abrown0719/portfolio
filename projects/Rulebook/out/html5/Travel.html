Travel

This is a description of long distance travel.

Between certain events, much of the time of adventuring and traveling is the actual journey itself. Characters may often spend weeks in an airship before they reach their target city, and upon another mission are sent to a location three days away. The journey itself is just as important as combat.

GM’s are responsible for pacing a game, and journeys don’t need to be noted or measured all the time, but the journey itself can be filled with such rich storytelling that it would be a shame to pass up the chance.

Long Distance Travel is determined by what kind of movement the characters are taking.

For every 2 levels of Athletics a character possesses, they add an extra mile onto their daily walking and marching distances.
