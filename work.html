Technical Writing and Projects

My undergraduate thesis is by far the most impressive and the most invovled writing project I have ever completed. Researched and written entirely by myself over the course of 18 weeks, the project attempts to trace the history and cultural importance of Dragon Magazine (a gaming magazine for tabletop RPG enthusiasts) as it relates to the role-playing game hobby and its parent company, TSR Inc.

My thesis is well researched and cited, with a lot of primary source references and written entirely in the Chicago writing style, standard for Historical research writing.

I produced some basic documentation for a simple but large open-source RESTful API based around the Pokemon games and universe. The PokeAPI has a ton of information on everything related to Pokemon.

This project required me to learn to document RESTful API's, what API's are, how they work and where they are used. I also learned to read XML and JSON structured data and how to write in the YAML format. I have used these skills to document this API and have also used two different tools for document presentation, ReDoc and Swagger UI.
